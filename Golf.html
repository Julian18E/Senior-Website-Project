Welcome to Golf


Golf is a sport played on a large outdoor course, typically consisting of 18 holes. The objective of the game is to hit a small ball with a club, aiming to get the ball into each hole in as few strokes as possible. Players must navigate various obstacles, such as sand traps and water hazards, while accounting for factors like wind and terrain. Golf requires skill, precision, and strategy, as players must carefully choose their shots and adjust their swings based on the conditions of the course. It is often considered a leisurely activity, but can also be highly competitive at both amateur and professional levels.
