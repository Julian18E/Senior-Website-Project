Welcome to Hockey


Hockey is a fast-paced, physical sport played on ice. Two teams of six players each compete to score goals by shooting the puck into the opposing team's net. The game is divided into three periods, each lasting 20 minutes, with a 15-minute intermission between the second and third periods. Players use sticks to move the puck around the rink, and are allowed to body check opponents in an attempt to gain possession of the puck. Penalties are assessed for various infractions, such as tripping or slashing, and result in a player being sent to the penalty box for a designated amount of time. Hockey requires skill, speed, endurance, and teamwork, making it a thrilling and challenging sport to watch and play.
